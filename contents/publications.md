- ATOM: A Framework of Detecting Query-Based Model Extraction Attacks for Graph Neural Networks

  **Zhan Cheng**, Bolin Shen, Tianming Sha, Yuan Gao, Shibo Li, Yushun Dong

  ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (**SIGKDD 2025**).

  A detection framework that identifies when adversaries attempt to steal GNN models through carefully crafted queries, without requiring knowledge of the attack strategy.
